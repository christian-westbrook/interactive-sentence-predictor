== Mythology ==
* [[Ajax (mythology)]], son of Telamon, ruler of Salamis and a hero in the Trojan War, also known as &quot;Ajax the Great(er)&quot;
* [[Ajax the Lesser]], son of Oileus, ruler of Locris and the leader of the Locrian contingent during the Trojan War
