'''Adder''' may refer to:

==Snakes==
* Any of several groups of venomous snakes of the [[Viperidae]] family
**''[[Vipera berus]]&lt;!--OK 2nd link--&gt;'', the common European adder, found in Europe and northern Asia
* ''[[Acanthophis|Acanthophis sp.]]'', death adders, elapid snakes found in Southeast Asia and Australia
* ''[[Heterodon|Heterodon sp.]]'', hog-nosed snakes, a genus of harmless colubrid snakes found in North America
* ''[[Agkistrodon contortrix mokasen]]'', the northern copperhead, a venomous viper found in the eastern US
