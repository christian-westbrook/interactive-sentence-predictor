==Events==
* [[708]] &amp;ndash; [[Wadōkaichin|Copper coins]] are minted in Japan for the first time (Traditional [[Japanese calendar|Japanese date]]: August 10, 708).
*[[1261]] &amp;ndash; [[Pope Urban IV]] succeeds [[Pope Alexander IV]] as the 182nd pope.
*[[1315]] &amp;ndash; [[Battle of Montecatini]]: The army of the [[Republic of Pisa]], commanded by [[Uguccione della Faggiuola]], wins a decisive victory against the joint forces of the [[Kingdom of Naples]] and the [[Republic of Florence]] despite being outnumbered.
*[[1350]] &amp;ndash; [[Battle of Winchelsea]] (or [[Les Espagnols sur Mer]]): The [[Kingdom of England|English]] [[naval fleet]] under King [[Edward III of England|Edward III]] defeats a [[Crown of Castile|Castilian]] fleet of 40 ships.
*[[1475]] &amp;ndash; The [[Treaty of Picquigny]] ends a brief war between  the kingdoms of [[Kingdom of France|France]] and England.
*[[1484]] &amp;ndash; [[Pope Innocent VIII]] succeeds [[Pope Sixtus IV]].
*[[1498]] &amp;ndash; [[Vasco da Gama]] decides to depart [[Kozhikode|Calicut]] and return to [[Kingdom of Portugal]].
*[[1521]] &amp;ndash; The [[Ottoman Turks]] capture [[Nándorfehérvár]] (Belgrade).
*[[1526]] &amp;ndash; [[Battle of Mohács]]: The [[Ottoman Turks]] led by [[Suleiman the Magnificent]] defeat and kill the last [[Jagiellonian]] king of [[Kingdom of Hungary|Hungary]] and [[Kingdom of Bohemia|Bohemia]].
*[[1541]] &amp;ndash; The [[Ottoman Turks]] capture [[Buda]], the capital of the [[Hungarian Kingdom]].
*[[1728]] &amp;ndash; The city of [[Nuuk]] in [[Greenland]] is founded as the fort of Godt-Haab by the [[list of governors of Greenland|royal governor]] [[Claus Paarss]].
*[[1756]] &amp;ndash; [[Frederick the Great]] attacks [[Electorate of Saxony|Saxony]], beginning the [[Seven Years' War]] in Europe.
*[[1758]] &amp;ndash; The [[Treaty of Easton]] establishes the first American [[Indian reservation]], at [[Indian Mills, New Jersey]], for the [[Lenape]].
*[[1778]] &amp;ndash; [[American Revolutionary War]]: British and American forces battle indecisively at the [[Battle of Rhode Island]].
*[[1786]] &amp;ndash; [[Shays' Rebellion]], an armed uprising of [[Massachusetts]] farmers, begins in response to high debt and tax burdens.
*[[1807]] &amp;ndash; British troops under Sir [[Arthur Wellesley, 1st Duke of Wellington|Arthur Wellesley]] defeat a Danish militia outside [[Copenhagen]] in the [[Battle of Køge]].
*[[1831]] &amp;ndash; [[Michael Faraday]] discovers [[electromagnetic induction]].
*[[1842]] &amp;ndash; [[Treaty of Nanking]] signing ends the [[First Opium War]].
*[[1861]] &amp;ndash; [[American Civil War]]: The [[Battle of Hatteras Inlet Batteries]] gives Federal forces control of [[Pamlico Sound]].
*[[1869]] &amp;ndash; The [[Mount Washington Cog Railway]] opens, making it the world's first mountain-climbing [[rack railway]].
*[[1871]] &amp;ndash; [[Emperor Meiji]] orders the [[abolition of the han system]] and the establishment of [[Prefectures of Japan|prefectures]] as local centers of administration. (Traditional [[Japanese calendar|Japanese date]]: July 14, 1871).
*[[1885]] &amp;ndash; [[Gottlieb Daimler]] patents the world's first [[internal combustion]] [[motorcycle]], the ''[[Daimler Reitwagen|Reitwagen]]''.
*[[1898]] &amp;ndash; The [[Goodyear Tire and Rubber Company|Goodyear]] tire company is founded.
*[[1903]] &amp;ndash; The {{ship|Russian battleship|Slava||2}}, the last of the five {{sclass-|Borodino|battleship}}s, is launched.
*[[1907]] &amp;ndash; The [[Quebec Bridge]] collapses during construction, killing 75 workers.
*[[1910]] &amp;ndash; The [[Japan–Korea Treaty of 1910]], also known as the Japan–Korea Annexation Treaty, becomes effective, officially starting the [[Korea under Japanese rule|period of Japanese rule]] in Korea.
*[[1911]] &amp;ndash; [[Ishi]], considered the last [[Native Americans in the United States|Native American]] to make contact with European Americans, emerges from the wilderness of northeastern [[California]].
*[[1914]] &amp;ndash; [[World War I]]: Start of the [[Battle of St. Quentin (1914)|Battle of St. Quentin]] in which the French [[Fifth Army (France)|Fifth Army]] counter-attacked the invading Germans at [[Saint-Quentin, Aisne]].
*[[1915]] &amp;ndash; US Navy salvage divers raise {{USS|F-4|SS-23|2}}, the first U.S. [[submarine]] sunk in an accident.
*[[1916]] &amp;ndash; The United States passes the [[Jones Law (Philippines)|Philippine Autonomy Act]].
*[[1918]] &amp;ndash; World War I: [[Bapaume]] taken by the [[New Zealand Division]] in the [[Hundred Days Offensive]].
*[[1930]] &amp;ndash; The last 36 remaining inhabitants of [[St Kilda, Scotland|St Kilda]] are voluntarily evacuated to other parts of Scotland.
*[[1941]] &amp;ndash; [[World War II]]: [[Tallinn]], the capital of [[Estonia]], is occupied by [[Nazi Germany]] following an occupation by the [[Soviet Union]].
*[[1943]] &amp;ndash; World War II: [[Occupation of Denmark|German-occupied Denmark]] scuttles most of its [[Royal Danish Navy|navy]]; Germany dissolves the Danish government.
*[[1944]] &amp;ndash; World War II:  [[Slovak National Uprising]] takes place as 60,000 [[Slovaks|Slovak]] troops turn against the [[Nazism|Nazi]]s.
*[[1949]] &amp;ndash; [[Soviet atomic bomb project]]: The [[Soviet Union]] tests its first [[atomic bomb]], known as ''[[First Lightning]]'' or ''Joe 1'', at [[Semipalatinsk Test Site|Semipalatinsk]], [[Kazakhstan]].
*[[1950]] &amp;ndash; [[Korean War]]: [[British people|British]] troops arrive in Korea to bolster the US presence there.
*[[1958]] &amp;ndash; [[United States Air Force Academy]] opens in [[Colorado Springs, Colorado]].
*[[1965]] &amp;ndash; The [[Gemini V]] spacecraft returns to [[Earth]], landing in the Atlantic Ocean.
*[[1966]] &amp;ndash; [[The Beatles]] perform their last concert before paying fans at [[Candlestick Park]] in [[San Francisco]].
*  1966   &amp;ndash; Leading Egyptian thinker [[Sayyid Qutb]] is executed for plotting the assassination of President [[Gamal Abdel Nasser]].
*[[1970]] &amp;ndash; [[Chicano Moratorium]] against the [[Vietnam War]], [[East Los Angeles, California]]. Police riot kills three people, including journalist [[Rubén Salazar]].
*[[1982]] &amp;ndash; The synthetic [[chemical element]] [[Meitnerium]], [[atomic number]] 109, is first synthesized at the [[Gesellschaft für Schwerionenforschung]] in [[Darmstadt]], Germany.
*[[1991]] &amp;ndash; [[Supreme Soviet of the Soviet Union]] suspends all activities of the [[Soviet Communist Party]].
*  1991   &amp;ndash; [[Libero Grassi]], an Italian businessman from [[Palermo]], is killed by the [[Sicilian Mafia]] after taking a solitary stand against their [[extortion]] demands.
*[[1996]] &amp;ndash; [[Vnukovo Airlines Flight 2801]], a [[Tupolev Tu-154]], crashes into a mountain on the [[Arctic]] island of [[Spitsbergen]], killing all 141 aboard.
*[[1997]] &amp;ndash; At least 98 villagers are killed by the Armed Islamic Group of Algeria [[Armed Islamic Group|GIA]] in the [[Rais massacre]], [[Algeria]].
*[[2003]] &amp;ndash; [[Ayatollah]] [[Sayed Mohammed Baqir al-Hakim]], the [[Shia Muslim]] leader in [[Iraq]], is [[assassination|assassin]]ated in a [[terrorism|terrorist]] bombing, along with nearly 100 worshippers as they leave a [[mosque]] in [[Najaf]].
*[[2005]] &amp;ndash; [[Hurricane Katrina]] [[Effects of Hurricane Katrina in New Orleans|devastates]] much of the [[U.S. Gulf Coast]] from [[Louisiana]] to the [[Florida Panhandle]], killing an estimated 1,836 people and causing over $108 billion in damage.
*[[2012]] &amp;ndash; At least 26 Chinese miners are killed and 21 missing after a blast in the [[Xiaojiawan coal mine disaster|Xiaojiawan coal mine]], located at [[Panzhihua|Panzhihua, Sichuan Province]].
