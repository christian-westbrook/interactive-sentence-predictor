==Events==
*[[AD 8]] &amp;ndash; [[Roman Empire]] [[General officer|general]] [[Tiberius]] defeats the [[Dalmatae]] on the river [[Bosna (river)|Bosna]]
*[[AD 70]] &amp;ndash; Fires resulting from the destruction of the [[Second Temple]] are extinguished.
* [[435]] &amp;ndash; Deposed [[Ecumenical Patriarch of Constantinople]] [[Nestorius]], considered the originator of [[Nestorianism]], is exiled by [[List of Byzantine emperors|Roman Emperor]] [[Theodosius II]] to a [[monastery]] in [[Egypt]].
* [[881]] &amp;ndash; [[Battle of Saucourt-en-Vimeu]]: [[Louis III of France]] defeats the [[Vikings]], an event celebrated in the poem ''[[Ludwigslied]]''.
* [[908]] &amp;ndash; [[Battle of Eisenach (908)|Battle of Eisenach]]: An invading [[Principality of Hungary|Hungarian]] force defeats an [[East Francia|East Frankish]] army under Duke [[Burchard, Duke of Thuringia|Burchard]] of [[Thuringia]].
*[[1031]] &amp;ndash; [[Olaf II of Norway]] is canonized as Saint Olaf by [[Grimketel]], the English [[Bishop of Selsey]].
*[[1057]] &amp;ndash; Frederik van Lotharingen elected as first Belgian [[Pope Stephen IX]].
*[[1342]] &amp;ndash; The [[Siege of Algeciras (1342–44)|Siege of Algeciras]] commences during the Spanish [[Reconquista]].
*[[1492]] &amp;ndash; [[Christopher Columbus]] sets sail from [[Palos de la Frontera]], Spain.
*[[1527]] &amp;ndash; The first known letter from North America is sent by [[John Rut]] while at [[St. John's, Newfoundland and Labrador|St. John's, Newfoundland]].
*[[1601]] &amp;ndash; [[Long Turkish War|Long War]]: [[Habsburg Monarchy|Austria]] captures [[Transylvania]] in the [[Battle of Goroszló]].
*[[1645]] &amp;ndash; [[Thirty Years' War]]: The [[Battle of Nördlingen (1645)|Second Battle of Nördlingen]] sees French forces defeating those of the [[Holy Roman Empire]].
*[[1678]] &amp;ndash; [[René-Robert Cavelier, Sieur de La Salle|Robert LaSalle]] builds the ''[[Le Griffon]]'', the first known ship built on the [[Great Lakes]].
*[[1778]] &amp;ndash; The theatre ''[[La Scala]]'' is inaugurated.
*[[1795]] &amp;ndash; [[Treaty of Greenville]] is signed, ending the [[Northwest Indian War]] in the [[Ohio Country]].
*[[1811]] &amp;ndash; First ascent of [[Jungfrau]], third highest summit in the [[Bernese Alps]] by brothers Johann Rudolf and Hieronymus Meyer.
*[[1852]] &amp;ndash; [[Harvard University]] wins the first [[Harvard–Yale Regatta|Boat Race]] between [[Yale University]] and Harvard. The race is also the first American intercollegiate athletic event
*[[1859]] &amp;ndash; The [[American Dental Association]] is founded in [[Niagara Falls, New York]].
*[[1900]] &amp;ndash; The [[Firestone Tire and Rubber Company]] is founded.
*[[1903]] &amp;ndash; [[Internal Macedonian Revolutionary Organization|Macedonian rebels]] in [[Kruševo]] proclaim the [[Kruševo Republic]], which exists only for ten days before [[Ottoman Empire|Ottoman Turks]] lay waste to the town.
*[[1907]] &amp;ndash; Judge [[Kenesaw Mountain Landis]] fines [[Amoco|Standard Oil of Indiana]] a record $29.4 million for illegal rebating to freight carriers; the conviction and fine are later reversed on appeal.
*[[1914]] &amp;ndash; [[World War I]]: Germany declares war against France, while [[Romania during World War I|Romania declares its neutrality]].
*[[1921]] &amp;ndash; [[Commissioner of Baseball|Major League Baseball Commissioner]] [[Kenesaw Mountain Landis]] confirms the ban of the eight [[Black Sox Scandal|Chicago Black Sox]], the day after they were acquitted by a Chicago court.
*[[1936]] &amp;ndash; [[Jesse Owens]] wins the 100 metre dash, defeating [[Ralph Metcalfe]], at the [[1936 Summer Olympics|Berlin Olympics]].
*  1936   &amp;ndash; A fire wipes out [[Kursha-2]] in the [[Meshchera Lowlands]], [[Ryazan Oblast]], [[Russian Soviet Federative Socialist Republic|Russia]], killing 1,200 and leaving only 20 survivors.
*[[1940]] &amp;ndash; [[World War II]]: [[Kingdom of Italy|Italian]] forces begin the [[Italian conquest of British Somaliland|invasion of British Somaliland]].
*[[1946]] &amp;ndash; [[Holiday World &amp; Splashin' Safari|Santa Claus Land]], the world's first themed [[amusement park]], opens in [[Santa Claus, Indiana]], United States.
*[[1948]] &amp;ndash; [[Whittaker Chambers]] accuses [[Alger Hiss]] of being a [[Communism|communist]] and a [[Espionage|spy]] for the [[Soviet Union]].
*[[1949]] &amp;ndash; The [[Basketball Association of America]] and the [[National Basketball League (United States)|National Basketball League]] finalize the merger, that would create the [[National Basketball Association]]
*[[1958]] &amp;ndash; US Nuclear submarine, Nautiluss, the first submarine to complete a submerged transit of the North Pole on 3 August 1958.
*[[1959]] &amp;ndash; Portugal's state police force [[PIDE]] [[Pidjiguiti massacre|fires upon striking workers]] in [[Bissau]], [[Portuguese Guinea]], killing over 50 people.
*[[1960]] &amp;ndash; [[Niger]] gains independence from France.
*[[1972]] &amp;ndash; The [[United States Senate]] ratifies the [[Anti-Ballistic Missile Treaty]].
*[[1975]] &amp;ndash; A privately chartered [[Boeing 707]] [[Agadir air disaster|strikes a mountain peak and crashes]] near [[Agadir]], [[Morocco]], killing 188.
*[[1977]] &amp;ndash; [[Tandy Corporation]] announces the [[TRS-80]], one of the world's first mass-produced [[personal computer]]s.
*[[1981]] &amp;ndash; [[Senegal]]ese opposition parties, under the leadership of [[Mamadou Dia]], launch the [[Antiimperialist Action Front – Suxxali Reew Mi]].
*[[1997]] &amp;ndash; [[Oued El-Had and Mezouara massacre]] in [[Algeria]]: A total of 116 villagers killed, 40 in Oued El-Had and 76 in Mezouara.
*[[2004]] &amp;ndash; The pedestal of the [[Statue of Liberty]] reopens after being closed since the [[September 11 attacks]].
*[[2005]] &amp;ndash; [[List of heads of state of Mauritania|President of Mauritania]] [[Maaouya Ould Sid'Ahmed Taya]] is overthrown in a [[Coup d'état|military coup]] while attending the funeral of [[Fahd of Saudi Arabia|King Fahd]] in [[Saudi Arabia]].
*[[2007]] &amp;ndash; Former Deputy Director of the [[Dirección de Inteligencia Nacional|Chilean secret police]] [[Raúl Iturriaga]] is captured after having been on the run following a conviction for [[kidnapping]].
*[[2010]] &amp;ndash; [[2010 Karachi riots|Widespread rioting]] erupts in [[Karachi]], [[Pakistan]], after the assassination of a local politician, leaving at least 85 dead and at least 17 billion [[Pakistani rupee]]s ([[United States dollar|US$]]200 million) in damage.
*[[2014]] &amp;ndash; A 6.1 magnitude [[2014 Ludian earthquake|earthquake]] kills at least 617 people and injures more than 2,400 in [[Yunnan]], China.
