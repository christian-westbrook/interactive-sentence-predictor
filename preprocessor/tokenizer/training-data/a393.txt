This article is about communications systems in [[Antigua and Barbuda]].

==Telephone==
'''Telephones – main lines in use:''' 37,500 (2006)
:''country comparison to the world:'' 168

'''Telephones – mobile cellular:''' 110,200 (2006) (APUA PCS, Cable &amp; Wireless, Digicel)

'''Telephone system:'''
&lt;br&gt;''domestic:'' good automatic telephone system
&lt;br&gt;''international:'' 3 fiber optic submarine cables (2 to [[St. Kitts]] and 1 to [[Guadeloupe]]); satellite earth station – 1 Intelsat (Atlantic Ocean)
