==Events==
* [[526]] &amp;ndash; King [[Theoderic the Great]] dies of [[dysentery]] at [[Ravenna]]; his daughter [[Amalasuntha]] takes power as [[Regency|regent]] for her 10-year-old son [[Athalaric]].
*[[1282]] &amp;ndash; [[Peter III of Aragon]] lands at [[Trapani]] to intervene in the [[War of the Sicilian Vespers]].
*[[1363]] &amp;ndash; The five-week [[Battle of Lake Poyang]] begins, in which the forces of two Chinese rebel leaders ([[Chen Youliang]] and [[Zhu Yuanzhang]]) meet to decide who will supplant the [[Yuan dynasty]].
*[[1464]] &amp;ndash; [[Pope Paul II]] succeeds [[Pope Pius II]] as the 211th [[pope]].
*[[1574]] &amp;ndash; [[Guru Ram Das]] becomes the Fourth [[Sikh Guru]]/[[guru|Master]].
*[[1590]] &amp;ndash; [[Tokugawa Ieyasu]] enters [[Edo Castle]]. (Traditional [[Japanese calendar|Japanese date]]: August 1, 1590)
*[[1727]] &amp;ndash; [[Anne, Princess Royal and Princess of Orange|Anne]], eldest daughter of King [[George II of Great Britain]], is given the title [[Princess Royal]].
*[[1791]] &amp;ndash; {{HMS|Pandora|1779|6}} sinks after having run aground on the outer [[Great Barrier Reef]] the previous day.
*[[1799]] &amp;ndash; The entire Dutch fleet [[Vlieter Incident|is captured]] by British forces under the command of Sir [[Ralph Abercromby]] and [[Admiral]] Sir Charles Mitchell during the [[War of the Second Coalition]].
*[[1800]] &amp;ndash; [[Gabriel Prosser]] postpones a planned [[slave rebellion]] in [[Richmond, Virginia]], but is arrested before he can make it happen.
*[[1813]] &amp;ndash; [[First Battle of Kulm]]: French forces are defeated by an Austrian-[[Prussia]]n-Russian alliance.
*  1813   &amp;ndash; [[Creek War]]: [[Fort Mims massacre]]: [[Creek people|Creek]] &quot;[[Red Sticks]]&quot; kill over 500 settlers (including over 250 armed militia) in Fort Mims, north of [[Mobile, Alabama]].
*[[1835]] &amp;ndash; Australia: [[Melbourne, Victoria]] is founded.
*[[1862]] &amp;ndash; [[American Civil War]]: [[Battle of Richmond]]: [[Confederate States of America|Confederates]] under [[Edmund Kirby Smith]] rout [[Union Army|Union]] forces under General [[William &quot;Bull&quot; Nelson]].
*[[1873]] &amp;ndash; Austrian explorers [[Julius von Payer]] and [[Karl Weyprecht]] discover the [[archipelago]] of [[Franz Josef Land]] in the Arctic Sea.
*[[1896]] &amp;ndash; [[Philippine Revolution]]: After Spanish victory in the [[Battle of San Juan del Monte]], eight provinces in the [[Philippines]] are declared under martial law by the Spanish [[Governor-General of the Philippines|Governor-General]] [[Ramón Blanco y Erenas]].
*[[1909]] &amp;ndash; [[Burgess Shale]] fossils are discovered by [[Charles Doolittle Walcott]].
*[[1914]] &amp;ndash; [[World War I]]: Germans defeat the Russians in the [[Battle of Tannenberg]].
*[[1917]] &amp;ndash; [[Vietnam]]ese prison guards led by Trịnh Văn Cấn [[Thái Nguyên uprising|mutiny]] at the [[Thái Nguyên]] penitentiary against local French authority.
*[[1918]] &amp;ndash; [[Fanni Kaplan]] shoots and seriously injures [[Bolshevik]] leader [[Vladimir Lenin]], which along with the assassination of [[Bolshevik]] senior official [[Moisei Uritsky]] days earlier, prompts the decree for [[Red Terror]].
*[[1922]] &amp;ndash; [[Battle of Dumlupınar]]: The final battle in the [[Greco-Turkish War (1919–22)|Greco-Turkish War]] (&quot;Turkish War of Independence&quot;).
*[[1940]] &amp;ndash; The [[Second Vienna Award]] reassigns the territory of [[Northern Transylvania]] from [[Romania]] to Hungary.
*[[1942]] &amp;ndash; World War II: The [[Battle of Alam el Halfa]] begins.
*[[1945]] &amp;ndash; Hong Kong is liberated from Japan by British Armed Forces.
*  1945   &amp;ndash; The [[Commander-in-chief#United States|Supreme Commander]] of the Allied Forces, General [[Douglas MacArthur]] lands at [[Atsugi Air Force Base]].
*  1945   &amp;ndash; The [[Allied Control Council]], governing Germany after World War II, comes into being.
*  1945   &amp;ndash; The [[August Revolution]] ends as Emperor [[Bảo Đại]] abdicates, ending the [[Nguyễn dynasty]].
*[[1962]] &amp;ndash; Japan conducts a test of the [[NAMC YS-11]], its first aircraft since World War II and its only successful commercial aircraft from before or after the war.
*[[1963]] &amp;ndash; The [[Moscow–Washington hotline]] between the leaders of the U.S. and the [[Soviet Union]] goes into operation.
*[[1967]] &amp;ndash; [[Thurgood Marshall]] is confirmed as the first [[African American]] [[Associate Justice of the Supreme Court of the United States|Justice]] of the [[Supreme Court of the United States]].
*[[1974]] &amp;ndash; A [[Belgrade]]–[[Dortmund]] express train [[Zagreb train disaster|derails]] at the main train station in [[Zagreb]] killing 153 passengers.
*  1974   &amp;ndash; A powerful bomb explodes at the [[Mitsubishi Heavy Industries]] headquarters in [[Marunouchi]], Tokyo. Eight are killed, 378 are injured. Eight left-wing activists are arrested on May 19, 1975 by Japanese authorities.
*[[1981]] &amp;ndash; President [[Mohammad-Ali Rajai]] and Prime Minister [[Mohammad-Javad Bahonar]] of Iran are assassinated in a bombing committed by the [[People's Mujahedin of Iran]].
*[[1984]] &amp;ndash; [[STS-41-D]]: The [[Space Shuttle]] ''[[Space Shuttle Discovery|Discovery]]'' takes off on its maiden voyage.
*[[1991]] &amp;ndash; [[Dissolution of the Soviet Union]]: [[Azerbaijan]] declares independence from Soviet Union.
*[[1992]] &amp;ndash; The 11-day [[Ruby Ridge]] standoff ends with [[Randy Weaver]] surrendering to federal authorities.
*[[1995]] &amp;ndash; [[Bosnian War]]: [[NATO]] launches [[Operation Deliberate Force]] against [[Bosnian Serb]] forces.
*[[1998]] &amp;ndash; [[Second Congo War]]: Armed forces of the [[Democratic Republic of the Congo]] (DRC) and their [[Angola]]n and [[Zimbabwe]]an allies recapture [[Matadi]] and the [[Inga dams]] in the western DRC from [[Rally for Congolese Democracy|RCD]] and [[Rwanda]]n troops.
*[[2014]] &amp;ndash; Prime Minister of Lesotho [[Tom Thabane]] flees to South Africa as the army [[2014 Lesotho political crisis|allegedly stages a coup]].
