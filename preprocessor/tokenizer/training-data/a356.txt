==Events==
* [[238]] &amp;ndash; [[Gordian II]] loses the [[Battle of Carthage (238)|Battle of Carthage]] against the [[Numidia]]n forces loyal to [[Maximinus Thrax]] and is killed. [[Gordian I]], his father, commits [[suicide]].
* [[467]] &amp;ndash; [[Anthemius]] is elevated to [[Roman emperor|Emperor of the Western Roman Empire]].
* [[627]] &amp;ndash; King [[Edwin of Northumbria]] is converted to Christianity by [[Paulinus of York|Paulinus]], bishop of [[York]].
*[[1167]] &amp;ndash; King [[Karl Sverkersson]] of Sweden is murdered on [[Visingsö]].
*[[1204]] &amp;ndash; The Crusaders of the [[Fourth Crusade]] breach the walls of [[Constantinople]] and [[Siege of Constantinople (1204)|enter the city]], which they completely occupy the following day.
*[[1606]] &amp;ndash; The [[Union Flag]] is adopted as the flag of [[Kingdom of England|English]] and [[Kingdom of Scotland|Scottish]] ships.
*[[1776]] &amp;ndash; [[American Revolution]]: With the [[Halifax Resolves]], the [[North Carolina Provincial Congress]] authorizes its [[Second Continental Congress|Congressional]] delegation to vote for independence from Britain.
*[[1807]] &amp;ndash; The [[Froberg mutiny]] ends when the remaining mutineers blow up the magazine of [[Fort Ricasoli]].
*[[1820]] &amp;ndash; [[Alexander Ypsilantis]] is declared leader of [[Filiki Eteria]], a secret organization to overthrow [[Ottoman Empire|Ottoman]] rule over Greece.
*[[1831]] &amp;ndash; Soldiers marching on the [[Broughton Suspension Bridge]] in [[Manchester]], England cause it to collapse.
*[[1861]] &amp;ndash; [[American Civil War]]: [[Battle of Fort Sumter]]. The war begins with [[Confederate States of America|Confederate]] forces firing on [[Fort Sumter]], in the harbor of [[Charleston, South Carolina]].
*[[1862]] &amp;ndash; American Civil War: The Andrews Raid (the [[Great Locomotive Chase]]) occurs, starting from Big Shanty, Georgia (now [[Kennesaw, Georgia|Kennesaw]]).
*[[1864]] &amp;ndash; American Civil War: The [[Battle of Fort Pillow]]: [[Confederate States Army|Confederate]] forces kill most of the [[African American]] soldiers that surrendered at [[Fort Pillow]], [[Tennessee]].
*[[1865]] &amp;ndash; American Civil War: [[Mobile, Alabama]], falls to the [[Union (American Civil War)|Union Army]].
*[[1877]] &amp;ndash; The [[United Kingdom]] annexes the [[South African Republic|Transvaal]].
*[[1910]] &amp;ndash; {{SMS|Zrínyi}}, one of the last [[pre-dreadnought battleship]]s built by the [[Austria-Hungary|Austro-Hungarian]] Navy, is launched.
*[[1917]] &amp;ndash; [[World War I]]: [[Canadian Expeditionary Force|Canadian]] forces successfully complete the taking of [[Battle of Vimy Ridge|Vimy Ridge]] from the [[Germans]].
*[[1927]] &amp;ndash; [[Shanghai massacre of 1927]]: [[Chiang Kai-shek]] orders the [[Communist Party of China]] members executed in Shanghai, ending the [[First United Front]].
*  1927   &amp;ndash; [[Rocksprings, Texas]] was hit by an F5 tornado that destroyed 235 of the 247 buildings in the town and killed 72 townspeople and injured 205; third deadliest tornado in [[Texas]] history.
*[[1928]] &amp;ndash; The ''[[Bremen (aircraft)|Bremen]]'', a German [[Junkers W 33]] type aircraft, takes off for the first successful transatlantic aeroplane flight from east to west.
*[[1934]] &amp;ndash; The [[Wind speed#Highest speed|strongest surface wind]] gust in the world at the time of 231&amp;nbsp;mph, is measured on the summit of [[Mount Washington (New Hampshire)|Mount Washington]], [[New Hampshire]].  It has since been surpassed.
*  1934   &amp;ndash; The U.S. [[Auto-Lite strike]] begins, culminating in a five-day melee between [[Ohio National Guard]] troops and 6,000 [[strike action|strikers and picketers]].
*[[1937]] &amp;ndash; Sir [[Frank Whittle]] ground-tests the first [[jet engine]] designed to power an aircraft, at [[Rugby, Warwickshire|Rugby, England]].
*[[1945]] &amp;ndash; U.S. President [[Franklin D. Roosevelt]] dies in office; Vice President [[Harry S. Truman]] becomes President upon Roosevelt's death.
*  1945   &amp;ndash; The [[Ninth United States Army|U.S. Ninth Army]] under General [[William Hood Simpson|William H. Simpson]] crosses the [[Elbe|Elbe River]] astride [[Magdeburg]], and reached [[Tangermünde]]—only 50 miles from [[Berlin]].
*[[1955]] &amp;ndash; The [[polio vaccine]], developed by Dr. [[Jonas Salk]], is declared [[Jonas Salk#Test results announced|safe and effective]].
*[[1961]] &amp;ndash; The [[Soviet Union|Soviet]] [[cosmonaut]] [[Yuri Gagarin]] becomes the first human to travel into [[outer space]] and perform the first manned [[orbit]]al flight, [[Vostok 1]].
*[[1963]] &amp;ndash; The [[Soviet Union|Soviet]] nuclear-powered submarine [[Soviet submarine K-33|K-33]] collides with the Finnish merchant vessel M/S ''Finnclipper'' in the Danish straits.
*[[1970]] &amp;ndash; [[Soviet submarine K-8]], carrying four nuclear torpedoes, sinks in the [[Bay of Biscay]] four days after a fire on board.
*[[1980]] &amp;ndash; [[Samuel Doe]] takes control of [[Liberia]] in a [[coup d'état]], ending over 130 years of minority [[Americo-Liberian]] rule over the country.
*[[1981]] &amp;ndash; The first launch of a [[Space Shuttle]] (''[[Space Shuttle Columbia|Columbia]]'') takes place: The [[STS-1]] mission.
*[[1990]] &amp;ndash; [[Jim Gary]]'s &quot;Twentieth Century Dinosaurs&quot; exhibition opens at the [[Smithsonian Institution]] [[National Museum of Natural History]] in Washington, D.C. He is the only sculptor ever invited to present a [[solo show|solo exhibition]] there.
*[[1992]] &amp;ndash; The ''Euro Disney Resort'' officially opens with its theme park ''[[Disneyland Park (Paris)|Euro Disneyland]]''; the resort and its park's name are subsequently changed to [[Disneyland Paris]].
*[[1999]] &amp;ndash; United States President [[Bill Clinton]] is cited for [[contempt of court]] for giving &quot;intentionally false statements&quot; in a [[civil lawsuit]]; he is later fined and disbarred.
*[[2002]] &amp;ndash; A [[suicide bomber]] blows herself up at the entrance to Jerusalem's [[Mahane Yehuda Market]], killing seven people and wounding 104.
*[[2007]] &amp;ndash; A suicide bomber [[2007 Iraqi Parliament bombing|penetrates the Green Zone and detonates]] in a cafeteria within a parliament building, killing Iraqi MP [[Mohammed Awad]] and wounding more than twenty other people.
*[[2009]] &amp;ndash; [[Zimbabwe]] officially abandons the [[Zimbabwean dollar]] as its official currency.
*[[2013]] &amp;ndash; Two [[2013 Kidal suicide attack|suicide bombers]] kill three [[Military of Chad|Chadian soldiers]] and injure dozens of civilians at a market in [[Kidal|Kidal, Mali]].
*[[2014]] &amp;ndash; The [[Great Fire of Valparaíso]] ravages the Chilean city of [[Valparaíso]], killing 16, displacing nearly 10,000, and destroying over 2,000 homes.
*[[2017]] &amp;ndash; [[Zuma Must Fall]] protests resume in South Africa, with [[Julius Malema]] addressing large crowds in Pretoria.&lt;ref&gt;[http://weeklyxpose.co.za/2017/04/12/opposition-parties-take-their-grievance/ Weekly Xpose South Africa]&lt;/ref&gt;
