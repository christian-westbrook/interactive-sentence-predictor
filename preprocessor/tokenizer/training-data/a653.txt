It is the peak of the [[Perseids|Perseid meteor shower]]. It is also known as the &quot;[[Glorious Twelfth]]&quot; in the United Kingdom, as it marks the traditional start of the [[Driven grouse shooting|grouse shooting]] season.

==Events==
*[[1099]] &amp;ndash; [[First Crusade]]: [[Battle of Ascalon]] Crusaders under the command of [[Godfrey of Bouillon]] defeat [[Fatimid Caliphate|Fatimid]] forces led by [[Al-Afdal Shahanshah]]. This is considered the last engagement of the First Crusade.
*[[1121]] &amp;ndash; [[Battle of Didgori]]: The [[Georgia (country)|Georgian]] army under King [[David IV of Georgia|David IV]] wins a decisive victory over the famous [[Seljuq dynasty|Seljuk]] commander [[Ilghazi]].
*[[1164]] &amp;ndash; [[Battle of Harim]]: [[Nur ad-Din Zangi]] defeats the [[Crusades|Crusader]] armies of the [[County of Tripoli]] and the [[Principality of Antioch]].
*[[1323]] &amp;ndash; Signature of the [[Treaty of Nöteborg]] between Sweden and [[Novgorod Republic]], that regulates the border between the two countries for the first time.
*[[1492]] &amp;ndash; [[Christopher Columbus]] arrives in the [[Canary Islands]] on his first voyage to the New World.
*[[1499]] &amp;ndash; First engagement of the [[Battle of Zonchio]] between [[Republic of Venice|Venetian]] and [[Ottoman Empire|Ottoman]] fleets.
*[[1624]] &amp;ndash; The president of [[Louis XIII of France]]'s royal council is arrested, leaving [[Cardinal Richelieu]] in the role of the King's principal minister.
*[[1676]] &amp;ndash; [[Praying Indian]] [[John Alderman]] shoots and kills [[Metacomet]], the [[Wampanoag people|Wampanoag]] war chief, ending [[King Philip's War]].
*[[1687]] &amp;ndash; [[Battle of Mohács (1687)|Battle of Mohács]]: [[Charles V, Duke of Lorraine|Charles of Lorraine]] defeats the [[Ottoman Empire]].
*[[1765]] &amp;ndash; [[Treaty of Allahabad]] is signed. The Treaty marks the political and constitutional involvement and the beginning of [[Company rule in India]].
*[[1793]] &amp;ndash; The [[Rhône (department)|Rhône]] and [[Loire (department)|Loire]] ''[[Departments of France|départments]]'' are created when the former ''département'' of [[Rhône-et-Loire]] is split into two.
*[[1806]] &amp;ndash; [[Santiago de Liniers, 1st Count of Buenos Aires]] re-takes the city of [[Buenos Aires]], [[Argentina]] after the [[British invasions of the River Plate|first British invasion]].
*[[1831]] &amp;ndash; French intervention forces [[William I of the Netherlands]] to abandon [[Ten Days' Campaign|his attempt]] to suppress the [[Belgian Revolution]].
*[[1851]] &amp;ndash; [[Isaac Singer]] is granted a patent for his [[sewing machine]].
*[[1865]] &amp;ndash; [[Joseph Lister]], British surgeon and scientist,  performs 1st antiseptic surgery.
*[[1883]] &amp;ndash; The last [[quagga]] dies at the [[Natura Artis Magistra]], a zoo in [[Amsterdam]], Netherlands.
*[[1898]] &amp;ndash; The [[Flag of Hawaii|Hawaiian flag]] is lowered from [[ʻIolani Palace]] in an elaborate annexation ceremony and replaced with the [[flag of the United States]] to signify the transfer of sovereignty from the [[Republic of Hawaii]] to the United States.
*[[1914]] &amp;ndash; [[World War I]]: The United Kingdom declares war on [[Austria-Hungary]]; the countries of the [[British Empire]] follow suit.
*  1914   &amp;ndash; World War I: The [[Battle of Halen]] a.k.a. Battle of the Silver Helmets a clash between large Belgian and German [[cavalry]] formations at [[Halen]], Belgium.
*[[1944]] &amp;ndash; [[Waffen-SS]] troops massacre 560 people in [[Sant'Anna di Stazzema massacre|Sant'Anna di Stazzema]].
*  1944   &amp;ndash; [[Nazi Germany|Nazi German]] troops end the week-long [[Wola massacre]], during which time at least 40,000 people were killed indiscriminately or in mass executions.
*  1944   &amp;ndash; [[Alençon]] is liberated by General [[Philippe Leclerc de Hauteclocque]], the first city in France to be liberated from the [[Nazism|Nazis]] by French forces.
*[[1950]] &amp;ndash; [[Korean War]]: [[Bloody Gulch massacre]]: American POWs are massacred by [[Korean People's Army|North Korean Army]].
*[[1952]] &amp;ndash; The [[Night of the Murdered Poets]]: Thirteen prominent Jewish intellectuals are murdered in Moscow, Russia, Soviet Union.
*[[1953]] &amp;ndash; The first [[Nuclear weapons testing|testing]] of a real [[thermonuclear weapon]] (not test devices): The [[Soviet atomic bomb project]] continues with the detonation of &quot;RDS-6s&quot; (''[[Joe 4]]''), the first Soviet thermonuclear bomb.
*  1953   &amp;ndash; The 7.2 {{M|s}} [[1953 Ionian earthquake|Ionian earthquake]] shakes the southern [[Ionian Islands]] with a maximum [[Mercalli intensity scale|Mercalli intensity]] of X (''Extreme''). Between 445 and 800 people were killed.
*[[1960]] &amp;ndash; ''[[Echo 1A]]'', NASA's first successful [[communications satellite]], is launched.
*[[1964]] &amp;ndash; [[South Africa]] is banned from the [[Olympic Games]] due to the country's [[Apartheid in South Africa|racist policies]].
*[[1969]] &amp;ndash; Violence erupts after the [[Apprentice Boys of Derry]] march in [[Derry|Derry, Northern Ireland]], resulting in a three-day communal riot known as the [[Battle of the Bogside]].
*[[1976]] &amp;ndash; Between 1,000 and 3,500 [[Palestinians]] are killed in the [[Tel al-Zaatar massacre]], one of the bloodiest events of the [[Lebanese Civil War]]
*[[1977]] &amp;ndash; The first [[Approach and Landing Tests|free flight]] of the {{ship|Space Shuttle|Enterprise}}.
*  1977   &amp;ndash; The [[1977 riots in Sri Lanka|Sri Lanka Riots]]:, targeting the minority [[Sri Lanka]]n [[Tamils]], begin, less than a month after the [[United National Party]] came to power. Over 300 Tamils are killed.
*[[1981]] &amp;ndash; The [[IBM Personal Computer]] is released.
*[[1985]] &amp;ndash; [[Japan Airlines Flight 123]] crashes into [[Mount Osutaka|Osutaka]] ridge in [[Gunma Prefecture]], Japan, killing 520, to become the worst single-plane air disaster.
*[[1990]] &amp;ndash; [[Sue (dinosaur)|Sue]], the largest and most complete ''[[Tyrannosaurus Rex]]'' skeleton found to date, is discovered by [[Sue Hendrickson]] in [[South Dakota]].
*[[1992]] &amp;ndash; Canada, Mexico and the United States announce completion of negotiations for the [[North American Free Trade Agreement]] (NAFTA).
*[[1994]] &amp;ndash; [[Major League Baseball]] players go on [[1994–95 Major League Baseball strike|strike]], forcing the cancellation of the [[1994 World Series]].
*[[2000]] &amp;ndash; The [[Russian Navy]] submarine {{ship|Russian submarine|Kursk|K-141|2}} [[Kursk submarine disaster|explodes and sinks]] in the [[Barents Sea]] during a [[military exercise]], killing her entire 118-man crew.
*[[2015]] &amp;ndash; At least [[2015 Tianjin explosions|two massive explosions]] kill 173 people and injure nearly 800 more in [[Tianjin]], China.
