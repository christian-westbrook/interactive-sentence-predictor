'''Alexius''' is the [[Latinization (literature)|Latinized]] form of the given name '''Alexios''' ({{lang-el|Αλέξιος}}, polytonic {{lang|grc|Ἀλέξιος}}, &quot;defender&quot;, cf. [[Alexander]]), especially common in the later [[Byzantine Empire]]. Variants include '''[[Alexis (disambiguation)|Alexis]]''' with the Russian '''[[Alexey|Aleksey]]''' and its Ukrainian counterpart '''Oleksa/Oleksiy''' deriving from this form. The female form is '''[[Alexia (given name)|Alexia]]''' ({{lang-el|Αλεξία}}) and its variants such as [[Alessia]] (which male form is [[Alessio (disambiguation)|Alessio]]) in Italian.
