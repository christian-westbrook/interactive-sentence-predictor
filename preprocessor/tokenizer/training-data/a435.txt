==Events==
*[[338 BC]] &amp;ndash; A [[Ancient Macedonian army|Macedonian army]] led by [[Philip II of Macedon|Philip II]] defeated the combined forces of [[Athens]] and [[Thebes, Greece|Thebes]] in the [[Battle of Chaeronea (338 BC)|Battle of Chaeronea]], securing [[Macedonia (ancient kingdom)|Macedonian]] [[hegemony]] in Greece and the [[Aegean civilizations|Aegean]].
*[[216 BC]] &amp;ndash; The [[Carthage|Carthaginian]] army led by [[Hannibal]] defeats a numerically superior [[Roman Republic|Roman]] army at the [[Battle of Cannae]].
*[[AD 70]]  &amp;ndash; The armies of [[Titus]] destroy the [[Second Temple]] as the final blow of the [[Siege of Jerusalem (AD 70)|Siege of Jerusalem]].
* [[461]] &amp;ndash; [[Majorian]] is arrested near [[Tortona]] (northern Italy) and deposed by the [[Suebi]]an general [[Ricimer]] as [[Puppet monarch|puppet emperor]].
*[[1274]] &amp;ndash; [[Edward I of England]] returns from the [[Ninth Crusade]] and is crowned King seventeen days later.
*[[1343]] &amp;ndash; After the execution of her husband, [[Jeanne de Clisson]] sells her estates and raises a force of men with which to attack French shipping and ports.
*[[1377]] &amp;ndash; Russian troops are defeated by forces of the [[Blue Horde]] [[Khan (title)|Khan]] Arapsha in the [[Battle on Pyana River]].
*[[1415]] &amp;ndash; [[Thomas Grey (1384–1415)|Thomas Grey]], conspirator against King Henry V, beheaded.
*[[1610]] &amp;ndash; During [[Henry Hudson]]'s search for the [[Northwest Passage]], he sails into what is now known as [[Hudson Bay]].
*[[1776]] &amp;ndash; The signing of the [[United States Declaration of Independence]] took place.
*[[1790]] &amp;ndash; The first [[United States Census]] is conducted.
*[[1798]] &amp;ndash; [[French Revolutionary Wars]]: The [[Battle of the Nile]] concludes in a British victory.
*[[1830]] &amp;ndash; [[Charles X of France]] abdicates the throne in favor of his grandson [[Henri, Count of Chambord|Henri]].
*[[1869]] &amp;ndash; Japan's [[Edo society]] class system is abolished as part of the [[Meiji Restoration]] reforms.
*[[1870]] &amp;ndash; [[Tower Subway]], the world's first [[rapid transit|underground tube railway]], opens in London, England, United Kingdom.
*[[1873]] &amp;ndash; The [[Clay Street Hill Railroad]] begins operating the first [[cable car (railway)|cable car]] in San Francisco's famous [[San Francisco cable car system|cable car system]].
*[[1897]] &amp;ndash; [[Anglo-Afghan War]]: The [[Siege of Malakand]] ends when a relief column is able to reach the [[British Indian Army|British]] garrison in the [[Malakand Agency|Malakand states]].
*[[1903]] &amp;ndash; The [[Ilinden–Preobrazhenie Uprising]] against the [[Ottoman Empire]] begins.
*[[1914]] &amp;ndash; The [[German occupation of Luxembourg during World War I]] begins.
*[[1916]] &amp;ndash; [[World War I]]: [[Austria-Hungary|Austrian]] sabotage causes the sinking of the Italian battleship ''[[Italian battleship Leonardo da Vinci|Leonardo da Vinci]]'' in [[Taranto]].
*[[1918]] &amp;ndash; The first [[general strike]] in Canadian history [[1918 Vancouver general strike|takes place]] in Vancouver.
*[[1922]] &amp;ndash; A [[1922 Swatow typhoon|typhoon]] hits [[Shantou]], [[Taiwan|Republic of China]] killing more than 50,000 people.
*[[1923]] &amp;ndash; Vice President [[Calvin Coolidge]] becomes [[President of the United States|U.S. President]] upon the death of President [[Warren G. Harding]].
*[[1932]] &amp;ndash; The [[positron]] ([[antiparticle]] of the [[electron]]) is discovered by [[Carl David Anderson|Carl D. Anderson]].
*[[1934]] &amp;ndash; [[Gleichschaltung]]: [[Adolf Hitler]] becomes ''[[Führer]]'' of Germany following the death of President [[Paul von Hindenburg]].
*[[1937]] &amp;ndash; The [[Marihuana Tax Act of 1937]] is passed in America, the effect of which is to render [[cannabis (drug)|marijuana]] and all its by-products illegal.
*[[1939]] &amp;ndash; [[Albert Einstein]] and [[Leo Szilard]] write [[Einstein–Szilárd letter|a letter]] to [[Franklin D. Roosevelt]], urging him to begin the [[Manhattan Project]] to develop a nuclear weapon.
*[[1943]] &amp;ndash; Jewish prisoners stage a revolt at [[Treblinka extermination camp|Treblinka]], one of the deadliest of [[Nazism|Nazi]] [[Extermination camp|death camps]] where approximately 900,000 persons were murdered in less than 18 months.
*  1943   &amp;ndash; [[World War II]]: The [[Motor Torpedo Boat PT-109]] is rammed by the [[Imperial Japanese Navy|Japanese]] destroyer ''[[Japanese destroyer Amagiri (1930)|Amagiri]]'' and sinks. Lt. [[John F. Kennedy]], future U.S. President, saves all but two of his crew.
*[[1944]] &amp;ndash; [[ASNOM]]: Birth of the [[Socialist Republic of Macedonia]], celebrated as [[Republic Day (Republic of Macedonia)|Day of the Republic]] in the [[Republic of Macedonia]].
*  1944   &amp;ndash; World War II: The [[Convoy HX 300|largest trade convoy]] of the world wars arrives safely in the [[Western Approaches]].
*[[1945]] &amp;ndash; World War II: End of the [[Potsdam Conference]].
*[[1947]] &amp;ndash; A [[British South American Airways]] [[Avro Lancastrian]] airliner [[1947 BSAA Avro Lancastrian Star Dust accident|crashes into a mountain]] during a flight from [[Buenos Aires|Buenos Aires, Argentina]] to [[Santiago, Chile]]. The wreckage would not be found until 1998.
*[[1968]] &amp;ndash; An [[1968 Casiguran earthquake|earthquake]] hits [[Casiguran, Aurora]], [[Philippines]] killing more than 270 people and wounding 261.
*[[1973]] &amp;ndash; A flash fire kills 51 people at the [[Summerland disaster|Summerland]] amusement centre at [[Douglas, Isle of Man]].
*[[1980]] &amp;ndash; A [[Bologna massacre|bomb explodes]] at the [[Bologna Centrale railway station|railway station in Bologna, Italy]], killing 85 people and wounding more than 200.
*[[1985]] &amp;ndash; [[Delta Air Lines Flight 191]], a [[Lockheed L-1011 TriStar]], crashes at [[Dallas/Fort Worth International Airport]] killing 137.
*[[1989]] &amp;ndash; [[Pakistan]] is re-admitted to the [[Commonwealth of Nations]] after having restored democracy for the first time since 1972.
*  1989   &amp;ndash; A [[1989 Valvettiturai massacre|massacre]] is carried out by an [[Indian Peace Keeping Force]] in [[Sri Lanka]] killing 64 ethnic [[Tamils|Tamil]] civilians.
*[[1990]] &amp;ndash; [[Invasion of Kuwait|Iraq invades Kuwait]], eventually leading to the [[Gulf War]].
*[[1999]] &amp;ndash; The [[Gaisal train disaster]] claims 285 lives in [[Assam]], India.
*[[2005]] &amp;ndash; [[Air France Flight 358]] lands at [[Toronto Pearson International Airport]] and runs off the runway, causing the plane to burst into flames leaving 12 injuries and no fatalities.
*[[2014]] &amp;ndash; At least 146 people were killed and more than 114 injured in a [[2014 Kunshan explosion|factory explosion in Kunshan, Jiangsu, China]].
