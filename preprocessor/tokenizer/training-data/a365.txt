==Events==
* [[135]] &amp;ndash; The Roman Empire lays siege to [[Betar (fortress)#Fall of Betar|Betar]], effectively ending the [[Bar Kokhba revolt]].
*[[1284]] &amp;ndash; The [[Republic of Pisa]] is defeated in the [[Battle of Meloria (1284)|Battle of Meloria]] by the [[Republic of Genoa]], thus losing its naval dominance in the Mediterranean.
*[[1506]] &amp;ndash; The [[Grand Duchy of Lithuania]] defeated the [[Crimean Khanate]] in the [[Battle of Kletsk]]
*[[1538]] &amp;ndash; [[Bogotá]], [[Colombia]], is founded by [[Gonzalo Jiménez de Quesada]].
*[[1661]] &amp;ndash; The [[Treaty of The Hague (1661)|Treaty of The Hague]] is signed by Portugal and the [[Dutch Republic]].
*[[1777]] &amp;ndash; [[American Revolutionary War]]: The bloody [[Battle of Oriskany]] prevents American relief of the [[Siege of Fort Stanwix]].
*[[1787]] &amp;ndash; Sixty proof sheets of the [[United States Constitution|Constitution of the United States]] are delivered to the [[Constitutional Convention (United States)|Constitutional Convention]] in [[Philadelphia|Philadelphia, Pennsylvania]].
*[[1806]] &amp;ndash; [[Francis II, Holy Roman Emperor|Francis II]], the last [[Holy Roman Emperor]], abdicates, ending the [[Holy Roman Empire]].
*[[1819]] &amp;ndash; [[Norwich University]] is founded in [[Vermont]] as the first private military school in the United States.
*[[1824]] &amp;ndash; [[Battle of Junín]] [[Peru]].
*[[1825]] &amp;ndash; [[Bolivia]] gains independence from Spain.
*[[1861]] &amp;ndash; The United Kingdom annexes [[Lagos]], [[Nigeria]].
*[[1862]] &amp;ndash; [[American Civil War]]: The [[Confederate States of America|Confederate]] [[Ironclad warship|ironclad]] {{Ship|CSS|Arkansas}} is scuttled on the [[Mississippi River]] after suffering catastrophic engine failure near [[Baton Rouge, Louisiana]].
*[[1870]] &amp;ndash; [[Franco-Prussian War]]: The [[Battle of Spicheren]] is fought, resulting in a [[Kingdom of Prussia|Prussian]] victory.
*  1870   &amp;ndash; Franco-Prussian War: The [[Battle of Wörth]] results in a decisive [[Kingdom of Prussia|Prussian]] victory.
*[[1890]] &amp;ndash; At [[Auburn Correctional Facility|Auburn Prison]] in New York, murderer [[William Kemmler]] becomes the first person to be [[Capital punishment|executed]] by [[electric chair]].
*[[1901]] &amp;ndash; [[Kiowa]] land in [[Oklahoma]] is opened for white settlement, effectively dissolving the contiguous [[Indian reservation|reservation]].
*[[1912]] &amp;ndash; The [[Progressive Party (United States, 1912)|Bull Moose Party]] meets at the [[Chicago Coliseum]].
*[[1914]] &amp;ndash; [[World War I]]: [[First Battle of the Atlantic]]: Two days after the United Kingdom had declared war on Germany over the German invasion of Belgium, ten German [[U-boat]]s leave their base in [[Heligoland]] to attack [[Royal Navy]] warships in the [[North Sea]].
*  1914   &amp;ndash; World War I: [[Serbia]] declares war on Germany; Austria declares war on Russia.
*[[1915]] &amp;ndash; World War I: [[Battle of Sari Bair]]: The [[Allies of World War I|Allies]] mount a diversionary attack timed to coincide with a major Allied landing of reinforcements at [[Suvla|Suvla Bay]].
*[[1917]] &amp;ndash; World War I: [[Battle of Mărășești]] between the [[Romania]]n and German armies begins.
*[[1926]] &amp;ndash; [[Gertrude Ederle]] becomes the first woman to swim across the [[English Channel]].
*  1926   &amp;ndash; In New York City, the [[Warner Bros.]]' [[Vitaphone]] system premieres with the movie ''[[Don Juan (1926 film)|Don Juan]]'' starring [[John Barrymore]].
*[[1930]] &amp;ndash; Judge [[Joseph Force Crater]] steps into a taxi in New York and disappears never to be seen again.
*[[1940]] &amp;ndash; [[Estonia]] was illegally annexed by the [[Soviet Union]].
*[[1942]] &amp;ndash; Queen [[Wilhelmina of the Netherlands]] becomes the first reigning queen to address a joint session of the [[United States Congress]].
*[[1944]] &amp;ndash; The [[Warsaw Uprising]] occurs on [[August 1]]. It is brutally suppressed and all able-bodied men in Kraków are detained afterwards to prevent a similar uprising, the [[Kraków Uprising (1944)|Kraków Uprising]], that was planned but never carried out.
*[[1945]] &amp;ndash; [[World War II]]: [[Hiroshima]], Japan is [[Atomic bombings of Hiroshima and Nagasaki|devastated]] when the [[nuclear weapon|atomic bomb]] &quot;[[Little Boy]]&quot; is dropped by the United States [[Boeing B-29 Superfortress|B-29]] ''[[Enola Gay]]''. Around 70,000 people are killed instantly, and some tens of thousands die in subsequent years from burns and [[Acute radiation syndrome|radiation poisoning]].
*[[1956]] &amp;ndash; After going bankrupt in [[1955]], the American broadcaster [[DuMont Television Network]] makes its final broadcast, a boxing match from [[St. Nicholas Rink|St. Nicholas Arena]] in New York in the ''[[Boxing from St. Nicholas Arena]]'' series.
*[[1960]] &amp;ndash; [[Cuban Revolution]]: [[Cuba]] nationalizes American and foreign-owned property in the nation.
*[[1962]] &amp;ndash; [[Jamaica]] becomes independent from the United Kingdom.
*[[1965]] &amp;ndash; US President [[Lyndon B. Johnson]] signs the [[Voting Rights Act of 1965]] into law.
*[[1986]] &amp;ndash; A low-pressure system that redeveloped off the [[New South Wales]] coast dumps a record 328 millimeters (13&amp;nbsp;inches) of rain in a day on [[Sydney|Sydney, New South Wales]], Australia.
*[[1990]] &amp;ndash; [[Gulf War]]: The [[United Nations Security Council]] orders a global [[Embargo|trade embargo]] against [[Iraq]] in response to Iraq's invasion of [[Kuwait]].
*[[1991]] &amp;ndash; [[Tim Berners-Lee]] releases files describing his idea for the [[World Wide Web]]. WWW debuts as a publicly available service on the [[Internet]].
*  1991   &amp;ndash; [[Takako Doi]], chair of the [[Social Democratic Party (Japan)|Social Democratic Party]], becomes Japan's first female speaker of the [[House of Representatives (Japan)|House of Representatives]].
*[[1996]] &amp;ndash; [[The Ramones]] played their farewell concert at The Palace, Los Angeles, CA.
*  1996   &amp;ndash; [[NASA]] announces that the [[Allan Hills 84001|ALH 84001]] meteorite, thought to originate from [[Mars]], contains evidence of primitive life-forms.
*[[1997]] &amp;ndash; [[Korean Air Flight 801]] crashed at [[Nimitz Hill]], [[Guam]] killing 228 of 254 people on board.&lt;!--Resulted in notable deaths--&gt;
*[[2001]] &amp;ndash; [[Erwadi fire incident]], 28 mentally ill persons tied to a chain were burnt to death at a faith based institution at [[Erwadi]], [[Tamil Nadu]].
*[[2008]] &amp;ndash; A military junta led by [[Mohamed Ould Abdel Aziz]] stages a [[2008 Mauritanian coup d'état|coup d'état]] in [[Mauritania]], overthrowing president [[Sidi Ould Cheikh Abdallahi]].
*[[2010]] &amp;ndash; [[2010 Ladakh floods|Flash floods]] across a large part of [[Jammu and Kashmir]], India, damages 71 towns and kills at least 255 people.
*  2011   &amp;ndash; [[War in Afghanistan (2001–present)|War in Afghanistan]]: [[2011 Chinook shootdown in Afghanistan|A United States military helicopter is shot down]], killing 30 American special forces members and a working dog, 7 Afghan soldiers, and 1 Afghan civilian. It was the deadliest single event for the United States in the War in Afghanistan.
*[[2012]] &amp;ndash; [[NASA]]'s [[Curiosity (rover)|''Curiosity'' rover]] [[Mars Science Laboratory|lands]] on the surface of [[Mars]].
*[[2015]] &amp;ndash; A [[2015 Abha mosque bombing|suicide bomb attack]] kills at least 15 people at a mosque in the Saudi city of [[Abha]].
