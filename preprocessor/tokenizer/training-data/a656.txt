==Events==
* [[684]] &amp;ndash; [[Battle of Marj Rahit (684)|Battle of Marj Rahit]]: [[Umayyad]] partisans defeat the supporters of [[Ibn al-Zubayr]] and cement Umayyad control of [[al-Sham|Syria]].
*[[1304]] &amp;ndash; The [[Battle of Mons-en-Pévèle]] is fought to a draw between the [[Kingdom of France|French]] army and the [[County of Flanders|Flemish]] militias.
*[[1487]] &amp;ndash; The [[Siege of Málaga (1487)|Siege of Málaga]] ends with the taking of the city by [[Kingdom of Castile|Castilian]] and [[Kingdom of Aragon|Aragonese]] forces.
*[[1572]] &amp;ndash; Marriage in Paris, France, of the [[Huguenot]] King [[Henry IV of France|Henry III of Navarre]] to [[Margaret of Valois]], in a supposed attempt to reconcile [[Protestantism|Protestants]] and [[Catholic Church|Catholics]].
*[[1587]] &amp;ndash; [[Virginia Dare]], granddaughter of Governor [[John White (colonist and artist)|John White]] of the [[Roanoke Colony|Colony of Roanoke]], becomes the first English child born in the Americas.
*[[1590]] &amp;ndash; [[John White (colonist and artist)|John White]], the governor of the [[Roanoke Colony]], returns from a supply trip to England and finds his settlement deserted.
*[[1612]] &amp;ndash; The trial of the [[Pendle witches]], one of England's most famous witch trials, begins at [[Lancaster, Lancashire|Lancaster]] [[Assizes]].
*[[1634]] &amp;ndash; [[Urbain Grandier]], accused and convicted of sorcery, is burned alive in [[Loudun]], France.
*[[1783]] &amp;ndash; A [[1783 Great Meteor|huge fireball meteor]] is seen across Great Britain as it passes over the east coast.
*[[1838]] &amp;ndash; The [[United States Exploring Expedition|Wilkes Expedition]], which would explore the [[Puget Sound]] and Antarctica, weighs anchor at [[Hampton Roads]].
*[[1848]] &amp;ndash; [[Camila O'Gorman]] and Ladislao Gutierrez are executed on the orders of Argentine dictator [[Juan Manuel de Rosas]].
*[[1864]] &amp;ndash; [[American Civil War]]: [[Battle of Globe Tavern]]: [[Union (American Civil War)|Union]] forces try to cut a vital [[Confederate States of America|Confederate]] supply-line into [[Petersburg, Virginia]], by attacking the [[Wilmington and Weldon Railroad]].
*[[1868]] &amp;ndash; French [[astronomy|astronomer]] [[Pierre Janssen]] [[Timeline of chemical element discoveries|discovers]] [[helium]].
*[[1870]] &amp;ndash; [[Franco-Prussian War]]: [[Battle of Gravelotte]] is fought.
*[[1891]] &amp;ndash; [[1891 Martinique hurricane|Major hurricane]] strikes [[Martinique]], leaving 700 dead.
*[[1903]] &amp;ndash; German engineer [[Karl Jatho]] allegedly flies his self-made, motored gliding [[airplane]] four months before the first flight of the [[Wright brothers]].
*[[1917]] &amp;ndash; A [[Great Thessaloniki Fire of 1917|Great Fire]] in [[Thessaloniki]], Greece destroys 32% of the city leaving 70,000 individuals homeless.
*[[1920]] &amp;ndash; The [[Nineteenth Amendment to the United States Constitution]] is ratified, guaranteeing women's [[suffrage]].
*[[1938]] &amp;ndash; The [[Thousand Islands Bridge]], connecting New York, United States with [[Ontario]], Canada over the [[Saint Lawrence River]], is dedicated by U.S. President [[Franklin D. Roosevelt]].
*[[1940]] &amp;ndash; World War II: [[The Hardest Day]] air battle, part of the [[Battle of Britain]]. At that point, the largest aerial engagement in history with heavy losses sustained on both sides.
*[[1945]] &amp;ndash; [[Sukarno]] takes office as the [[List of Presidents of Indonesia|first president of Indonesia]], following the country's declaration of independence the previous day.
*[[1950]] &amp;ndash; [[Julien Lahaut]], the chairman of the [[Communist Party of Belgium]] is assassinated by far-right elements.
*[[1958]] &amp;ndash; [[Vladimir Nabokov]]'s controversial novel ''[[Lolita]]'' is published in the United States.
*  1958   &amp;ndash; [[Brojen Das]] from [[Bangladesh]] swims across the [[English Channel]] in a competition, as the first [[Bengalis|Bengali]] and the first Asian to do so. He came first among 39 competitors.
*[[1963]] &amp;ndash; [[Civil Rights Movement]]: [[James Meredith]] becomes the first African American to graduate from the [[University of Mississippi]].
*[[1965]] &amp;ndash; [[Vietnam War]]: [[Operation Starlite]] begins: [[United States Marine Corps|United States Marines]] destroy a [[Viet Cong]] stronghold on the Van Tuong peninsula in the first major American ground battle of the war.
*[[1966]] &amp;ndash; Vietnam War: The [[Battle of Long Tan]] ensues after a patrol from the [[6th Battalion, Royal Australian Regiment]] clashes with a [[Viet Cong]] force in [[Phước Tuy Province]].
*[[1971]] &amp;ndash; Vietnam War: Australia and New Zealand decide to withdraw their troops from [[Vietnam]].
*[[1976]] &amp;ndash; In the [[Korean Demilitarized Zone]] at [[Panmunjom]], the [[Axe murder incident]] results in the death of two US soldiers.
*[[1977]] &amp;ndash; [[Steve Biko]] is arrested at a police roadblock under the Terrorism Act No. 83 of 1967 in [[King William's Town]], South Africa.  He later dies from injuries sustained during this arrest bringing attention to South Africa's [[apartheid]] policies.
*[[1983]] &amp;ndash; [[Hurricane Alicia]] hits the [[Texas]] coast, killing 22 people and causing over US$1 billion in damage (1983 dollars).
*[[1989]] &amp;ndash; Leading presidential hopeful [[Luis Carlos Galán]] is assassinated near [[Bogotá]] in [[Colombia]].
*[[2003]] &amp;ndash; One year old [[Murder of Zachary Turner|Zachary Tuner]] is murdered in [[Newfoundland]] by his mother who was awarded custody despite facing trial for the murder of Zachary's father. The case led to reform of Canada's bail laws.
*[[2005]] &amp;ndash; A [[2005 Java–Bali blackout|massive power blackout]] hits the [[Indonesia]]n island of [[Java]], affecting almost 100 million people, [[List of major power outages#Largest|one of the largest and most widespread]] power outages in history.
*[[2008]] &amp;ndash; President of Pakistan [[Pervez Musharraf]] resigns under [[Movement to impeach Pervez Musharraf|threat of impeachment]].
*  2008   &amp;ndash; War of Afghanistan: [[Uzbin Valley ambush]] occurs.
